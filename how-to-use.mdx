PokeSignal is organized into five sequential sections that mirror the real-world journey of building an online Pokémon business. Each section builds on the last — concepts introduced in Foundations are applied in Sourcing, refined in Setup, and amplified in Growth. You can move through the course in order for the smoothest experience, or jump directly to the section most relevant to where you are right now.

## Navigating the Course

The sidebar on the left is your primary navigation tool. Every page lives inside a named section, and each section flows naturally into the next. If you already have inventory and just need to set up your store, go straight to **Setting Up Shop**. If you're brand new, start at the beginning — the Foundations section is short, but the mental models it builds will save you hours later.

<Note>
  If you're a complete beginner, follow the roadmap from start to finish before skipping around. The early modules answer questions you don't know you have yet — and skipping them is the most common reason new sellers make avoidable mistakes.
</Note>

## What Each Section Covers

<Accordion title="Foundations">
  The Foundations section gives you a working understanding of the Pokémon collectibles market before you spend any money. You'll learn how to read price trends, identify which product categories have the strongest demand, and choose a niche that matches your goals and budget. By the end, you'll have a clear picture of where you're entering the market and why.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Sourcing">
  Sourcing is where your profit is made or lost. This section covers every major buying channel — retail arbitrage, wholesale, local pickups, online marketplaces, and more — along with the frameworks you need to evaluate a deal in seconds. You'll also learn how to avoid the most common sourcing traps that drain new sellers' cash flow.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Setting Up Shop">
  Setting Up Shop walks you through choosing the right selling platform for your goals, building a storefront that earns buyer trust, and writing listings that rank well and convert. You'll also cover the operational basics — shipping, packaging, feedback management, and account health — so your business runs smoothly from day one.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Growing & Scaling">
  Once you're making consistent sales, this section shows you how to grow without burning out. You'll learn how to automate repetitive tasks, expand to additional channels, build a recognizable brand, and reinvest your profits in ways that compound over time. This is where a side hustle becomes a real business.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Resources">
  The Resources section is a living reference library. It contains pricing tools, supplier directories, platform comparison tables, spreadsheet templates, and community links. You'll return to it often as you work through the other sections — think of it as your quick-access toolbox.
</Accordion>

## Working Through the Material

Each page is designed to be actionable. Most modules end with a clear next step or a small task you can complete immediately. Treat those prompts seriously — students who take action between lessons progress significantly faster than those who read passively.

<Tip>
  Bookmark the **Resources** section early. It's packed with reference material — calculators, checklists, and supplier lists — that you'll want quick access to while you're working through the other modules.
</Tip>
