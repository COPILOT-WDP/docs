This roadmap gives you the full picture of your journey through PokeSignal — from understanding the market all the way to building a brand that scales. Each stage is designed to be completed in sequence, with every step laying the groundwork for the next. Use this page as your home base: return to it whenever you want to orient yourself, check your progress, or decide where to focus next.

## The Five-Stage Path

<Steps>
  <Step title="Understand the Market">
    Before you buy a single card or open a seller account, you need to understand the landscape you're entering. The [Pokémon Market Overview](/foundations/pokemon-market-overview) breaks down how the market is structured, what drives prices, and where the most reliable demand lives. This knowledge shapes every decision you'll make downstream.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Niche">
    The Pokémon market is vast — singles, sealed product, graded cards, vintage, modern, and more. [Choosing Your Niche](/foundations/choosing-your-niche) helps you narrow your focus to a segment that matches your starting budget, your interests, and the competitive reality of the market. A well-chosen niche lets you become an expert fast and build a loyal customer base.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Source Your Inventory">
    With your niche locked in, it's time to find product. [Where to Buy](/sourcing/where-to-buy) walks you through every major sourcing channel and teaches you how to evaluate deals so you only buy inventory that will actually sell at a profit. Reliable sourcing is the engine that keeps your business running.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Your Store">
    Now you're ready to sell. [Choosing a Platform](/setup/choosing-a-platform) guides you through comparing your options, setting up your storefront, and publishing listings that attract buyers and convert. You'll also cover the operational basics — shipping, packaging, and account health — so your launch goes smoothly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build & Scale">
    Once sales are flowing, shift your focus to long-term growth. [Building Your Brand](/growth/building-your-brand) covers automation, multi-channel expansion, community building, and reinvestment strategies that turn a working business into a thriving one. This is where the real leverage kicks in.
  </Step>
</Steps>
