## Your High-Level Journey

Every successful Pokémon seller follows the same core loop. Work through these four stages in order to build a business that runs reliably and grows over time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Research the Market">
    Learn which products are in demand, how prices move, and where the best opportunities exist right now. A solid research foundation prevents costly sourcing mistakes before you spend a single dollar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Source Your Inventory">
    Find trustworthy suppliers, evaluate deals with confidence, and build a repeatable purchasing process. Your ability to source smart is the single biggest driver of your profit margins.
  </Step>
  <Step title="List & Sell">
    Create listings that rank, convert, and earn you five-star feedback. You'll learn photography, pricing strategy, and the platform-specific rules that separate top sellers from the rest.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grow & Scale">
    Automate repetitive tasks, expand to new channels, and build a brand that buyers return to. Once your systems are in place, growth becomes a matter of turning up the volume.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You don't need to know everything before you start — you just need to start. Thousands of PokeSignal students made their first sale before finishing the course. Dive in, take action, and let momentum carry you forward.
</Tip>
